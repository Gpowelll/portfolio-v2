Welcome.

My name is Greg Powell and I live in Minneapolis, Minnesota. I moved here from Montana after living there my whole life two years ago. The Twin Cities are progressive, exciting, and filled with beautiful people.


I graduated with a bachelor's degree in Psychology and started a career in health and human services. Although I loved many aspects of that work, I soon discovered it wasn't for me in the long term. I think the skills I learned as well as the many challenging experiences encountered will benefit me in this new career.

About me

I have always had an interest in technology and the inner workings of programming. After taking an intro to web design course in the last semester of my undergrad, I became more intrigued. Afterwards, I wished I would have pursued computer science from the beginning. I have always loved art, building/creating things, and enjoying any process in which I can see the results of a process. I'm inspired after taking the leap towards this career change and having the chance to work on a team of other like-minded individuals. I enjoy working on the front end with CSS and HTML due to the creative energy it fulfills. I can spend hours changing and interacting with different fonts, color schemes, and layouts. I'm excited to be introduced to the other aspects of software engineering, which will expand what services I can further provide.

In my free time, I love fly fishing and being in nature, which was more common when I lived in Montana. I still like to get out when I can and check out local parks, lakes, and trails. I love to skateboard and cycle around the city. When I'm relaxing at home, I like to play video games, cook and watch various media with my partner.
